Frequently Asked Questions

Credit score requirements vary by lender, but generally a score of 660 or higher is considered good for car loans. However, we have financing options available for all credit levels, including special programs for those with less-than-perfect credit.

Down payment requirements vary by lender and loan type. Typically, you'll need between 10% and 20% of the car's purchase price. However, some lenders offer zero-down payment options for qualified buyers.

With our online application system, you can get pre-approved in as little as 24 hours. Once you've selected a vehicle, final approval typically takes 1-2 business days, depending on the lender and your specific situation.

Yes, we offer financing for both new and used vehicles. Used car loans may have slightly higher interest rates and shorter loan terms, but we have competitive options for quality pre-owned vehicles.

Most of our lending partners allow early repayment without penalties. Paying off your loan early can save you money on interest over the life of the loan. Be sure to check the specific terms of your loan agreement for details.
