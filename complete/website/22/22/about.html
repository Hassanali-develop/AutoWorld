Our Story

Driven by Passion, Powered by Trust

Founded in 2005, AutoWorld has grown from a small dealership to one of the most trusted names in the automotive industry. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide customers with a transparent, honest, and enjoyable car buying experience.

Over the years, we've expanded our inventory, services, and team, but our core values remain unchanged. We believe in building long-term relationships with our customers, founded on trust, integrity, and exceptional service.

Today, AutoWorld stands as a testament to what can be achieved when passion for automobiles meets a commitment to customer satisfaction. Our state-of-the-art showroom and dedicated team of professionals are here to help you find the perfect vehicle for your needs.

Our Journey

2005

AutoWorld Founded

AutoWorld was established with a vision to revolutionize the car buying experience through transparency and customer-centric service.

2008

First Expansion

Opened our second location in response to growing customer demand and expanded our inventory to include luxury vehicles.

2012

Warranty Program Launch

Introduced our comprehensive warranty program, providing customers with peace of mind and protection for their investments.

2015

Digital Transformation

Launched our online platform, allowing customers to browse inventory, schedule test drives, and complete financing applications online.
